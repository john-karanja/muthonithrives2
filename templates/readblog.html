Branching out: Stepping of the Matrix

Last week I touched on the subject of starting out, and how we should embrace something new as that is the only way we will grow. So today I want to discuss what I am going through as I branch out. I am slowly starting my side hustle and I have found a potential client for my services.The journey so far has been a doozy! I have gone from happy, ecstatic, scared and terrified so many times in the day that I am currently exhausted. “Yaaaaaas!!!! You are going to make it!” “Not bad, not bad.” “Are you sure?” “What if it doesn’t work out” “Nope, this is not it, I don’t think you are qualified for this” “You are not going to make it, you are not that good even” It has been a rollercoaster of emotions and I have been having difficulty keeping myself together. But this is what I have learnt: You have skills that are needed, wanted and will be rewarded by people out there. You are more than enough, you are qualified and capable. You must hold your head high, regardless of how you feel, people don’t buy from those who they sense are insecure, they buy from those who are sure of what they are selling. So for now, FAKE it till you make it. I however have decided that regardless of how I feel, I will believe in myself. I have drawn the line in the sand and I have decided to be positive, because being negative has gotten me here and here does not satisfy me anymore, I have lived the life of failure and average and it is because of being negative and not believing in myself that led me to accept less than I deserved.
